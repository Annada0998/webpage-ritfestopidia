Hello!

At MSRIT, sports is an integral part of the all-round development of the student's personality, and achieving excellence in sports is looked as a great way of boosting on morale and all round development of positive attitude. MSRIT is keen to implement programs to promote excellence in sports. At the forefront of the efforts, the Sports Department has several facilities. Through its various sporting events, MSRIT supports and nurtures the talent of youth; providing them with requisite infrastructure, equipment, coaching facilities and competition exposure.


VISION To embed students with life skills and prepare them to be fit and confident to take on the world


MISSION MSRIT Sports Department offers to manage, serve, promote various games both indoor and outdoor at all levels of play, thereby making the students to be lifetime learners in the classroom and on the field. To develop them spiritually into humans of character and godliness.
